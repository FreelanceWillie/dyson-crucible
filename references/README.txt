Reference images live here.

Drop 8 to 20 pictures that look the way you want YOUR art to look. Put your
everyday set right in the "default" folder. If you want a special look for one
asset, make a named subfolder (for example "warlock") and point a brief at it
with reference_set: warlock.

These images do two jobs:
  1. They teach the generator your style (via IP-Adapter).
  2. They are the yardstick every candidate is scored against (via CLIP).

Consistent style beats quantity. Ten pictures that share one clear look will
give you far better results than thirty that clash. Any common image format
(png, jpg, webp) is fine.
